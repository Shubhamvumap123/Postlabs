## 2024-05-18 - Fix custom dialog accessibility
**Learning:** Custom modals implemented with `createPortal` and `framer-motion` often miss critical accessibility attributes out of the box. Specifically, missing `role="dialog"`, `aria-modal="true"`, missing unique IDs for `aria-labelledby`/`aria-describedby` (which can be solved with React's `useId()`), and lack of global keyboard listeners (e.g. `document.addEventListener("keydown")` for closing on the `Escape` key) completely break screen reader and keyboard-only navigation.
**Action:** Always wrap custom modal portal contents with `role="dialog"` and `aria-modal="true"`. Dynamically generate IDs with `useId()` to link titles/descriptions to the dialog for screen readers, add `aria-label` to icon-only close buttons, ensure they have `:focus-visible` styles, and implement a global keydown listener to map `Escape` to the onClose action.
