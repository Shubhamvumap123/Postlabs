## 2025-02-12 - Loading state feedback on form submissions
**Learning:** Found that async form submissions (like newsletter signups in the footer) provide no visual feedback while the request is pending, leading to potential duplicate submissions or user confusion before the success/error toast appears.
**Action:** Always add explicit loading states (e.g., changing button text to "Submitting..." or showing a spinner) and disable form inputs/buttons during async operations to communicate system status clearly.
