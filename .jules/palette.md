## 2024-05-18 - Custom Modal Accessibility Mappings
**Learning:** Custom modal components (e.g., `Dialog.tsx`) require careful mapping of ARIA attributes (`role="dialog"`, `aria-modal="true"`) and dynamic IDs for `aria-labelledby` and `aria-describedby` utilizing `React.useId()`. Furthermore, global event listeners (like 'keydown' for 'Escape') should be attached to `document` rather than `window` to comply with stricter linting/Deno constraints and they require a separate `useEffect` independent from body scroll locking to avoid scrollbar flickering.
**Action:** When building or auditing custom modals, ensure `useId` is leveraged to map titles and descriptions appropriately. Always separate the side-effects of event listeners from DOM manipulation (like scroll locking) and bind generic browser events to `document`.
